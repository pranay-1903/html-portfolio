RHYS LARSEN'S PORTFOLIO

NAME: RHYS LARSEN

Ocupation: Body Guard

Book: Twisted Games

About :

“Bridget Von Ascheberg was mine and mine alone. It didn’t matter that she wasn’t mine to take. I was taking her anyway.”

Rhys Larsen is the main male lead of Twisted Games. He'd been assigned by the Royal Force to be Bridget von Ascheberg's bodyguard. His job has always been easy for him, until he met her. He went through a lot in his childhood, events that have scarred him for life.
